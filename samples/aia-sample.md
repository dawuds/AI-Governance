# Worked Example: Algorithmic Impact Assessment (HR-Recruitment AI)

> **AI Disclaimer:** This is a **SAMPLE** completed AIA for educational purposes. It follows the EU AI Act Article 27 and NGAIGE guidelines.
> **Audit Evidence Reference:** EU AI Act Art. 27 | Fundamental Rights Impact Assessment

## 1. System Description
*   **System Name:** HireSmart-v2
*   **Purpose:** Automated screening of CVs for high-volume recruitment.
*   **Risk Level:** HIGH (EU AI Act Annex III - Employment)

## 2. Assessment of Risks

| Risk Domain | Potential Impact | Mitigation Measure |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Bias/Fairness** | Gender bias in historical training data. | Synthetic data re-balancing and bias testing (80/20 rule). |
| **Transparency** | Candidates unaware of AI use. | Updated Privacy Notice with "HireSmart" disclosure. |
| **Oversight** | Over-reliance on AI scores. | HR Manager must review and override "No-Match" decisions. |

## 3. Data Governance
*   **Training Data:** Sourced from LinkedIn (Anonymized) and internal history.
*   **Privacy:** No biometric or sensitive health data used in screening logic.

## 4. Decision
**Recommended for Deployment:** YES (subject to quarterly bias audits).
